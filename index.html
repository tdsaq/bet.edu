Data Science at the University of Washington

Data Science Innovation

Through an evolution of the University of Washington eScience Institute in collaboration with the Center for Statistics and the Social Sciences, UW has become a national leader in advancing the techniques and technologies of data-intensive discovery and in putting these advances to work in a broad range of fields.

Big Data U

Through an NSF IGERT Award to create an innovative interdisciplinary Ph.D. program, a massively open online course, multiple certificate programs, and a catalog of data-focused courses, we are establishing curricula and programs to advance data science in and across every discipline on campus.
